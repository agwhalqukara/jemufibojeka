Donald Trump Calls Meghan McCain &quot;Angry and Obnoxious&quot;

August 2024 · 2 minute read

No holding back! Donald Trump took to Twitter on Saturday, Sept. 5, to let the world know his thoughts about conservative commentator Meghan McCain.

“@MeghanMcCain was terrible on @TheFive yesterday,” the GOP presidential front-runner wrote. “Angry and obnoxious, she will never make it on T.V. @FoxNews can do so much better!”

The real estate mogul’s harsh words come after McCain, 30 — the daughter of former Republican presidential nominee John McCain — made a guest appearance on The Five on Friday afternoon to discuss violence against police officers.

“I can’t believe I’m living in a society where it’s okay to call for violence against police officers,” she said at one point during the discussion.

McCain was nonplussed about Trump’s criticism, however, and even retweeted his words shortly thereafter.

“Oh man, I’ve officially made it in media now that @realDonaldTrump has officially attacked my appearance on @TheFive – thanks for watching!” she wrote.

Other prominent figures quickly tweeted their support for McCain.
