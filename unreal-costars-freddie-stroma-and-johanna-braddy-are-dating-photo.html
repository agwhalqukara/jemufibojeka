UnREAL Costars Freddie Stroma and Johanna Braddy Are Dating: Photo!

July 2024 · 2 minute read

Adam and Anna really ended up together!

UnREAL stars Freddie Stroma and Johanna Braddy are dating. Their Lifetime costar Constance Zimmer spilled the beans during a cast Periscope via Entertainment Weekly on Monday, Aug. 3.

The couple met on set of the addictive, borderline disturbing drama and have been dating for a few months. In July, Braddy snapped Instagram photos from Morocco, the location of Stroma’s new project, a Michael Bay film.

The British hunk, 28, and Braddy, 27, play bad boy suitor Adam Cromwell and Everlasting contestant Anna Martin, respectively, on The Bachelor-skewering series. And luckily for Braddy, Stroma doesn’t have any of Adam’s playboy tendencies.

“I’ve never been a player. I was at an all-boy boarding school, I’ve always kept my head down,” he told Us Weekly at UnREAL’s Paley Center panel on July 30. “I’ve never had much game.”

But despite Adam’s jerkiness, Stroma sees his good qualities. “He’s a bit of a jerk but he has pretty heroic moments I think,” he added of Adam. “…Which is why the show is so good. Each character does that, same with Rachel [Shiri Appleby] and Quinn [Zimmer] and everyone. They all have good moments, they have bad moments.”

Like Adam, he can keep a secret, however. “I am seeing someone right now,” the Harry Potter alum told Us coyly at the time, without giving a name.

UnREAL’s finale aired Aug. 3 and has been renewed for Season 2. Braddy costars in the ABC fall drama Quantico, premiering Sept. 27 at 10 p.m.
