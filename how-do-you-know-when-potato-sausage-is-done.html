How do I cook frozen potato sausage?

TO BOIL: Put frozen potato sausage in a pot and cover with water. Bring to a low boil and cook uncovered for approximately 30 minutes poking the casing occasionally with a fork to prevent the sausage from bursting. Can be fried or baked for a few minutes for brown, crispy casing.
