Kealia Ohai Wiki

Kealia Mae Ohai is an American Soccer player. She was born on January 31, 1992, in Draper, Utah, United States. Kealia is the caption of team Houston Dash. She is also a member of the United States women’s national soccer team. She became famous after scoring the single winning goal in the final match of the 2012 FIFA U-20 Women’s World Cup.

Her father name is Ben Ohai and mother name is Cindy Ohai. She has a sister named Megan. She did her schooling from Alta High School, Sandy, Utah, USA and college studies from the University of North Carolina. She earned Parade All-American honors in 2010. Kealia Ohai is legally blind in her right eye. Currently, she is in a relationship with American Football player J. J. Watt.
