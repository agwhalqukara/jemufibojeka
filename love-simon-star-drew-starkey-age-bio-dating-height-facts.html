Drew Starkey made a major impact in the acting industry after appearing in the 2018 romance movie titled Love, Simon. Eventually, the talented American actor made his mark with television shows like Outer Banks.

Drew Starkey Bio & Family; Age, Parents, Height, Ethnicity

Drew Starkey is 27 years of age and was born on 3 June 1993.

Starkey was raised by his parents, Todd Starkey and Jodi Ballard Hutto in North Carolina, the United States. Drew holds white ethnicity and his birth sign is Gemini.

Talking about his family, Drew shares a sibling bond with his brother, Logan Starkey, and his two sisters, Brooke Starkey and Mackayla Starkey. Logan is a musician and a guitar enthusiast.

Drew’s parents parted ways with a divorce after which his mother married Brian Hutto.

For his education, Drew attended St. Stephens High School. Eventually, he enrolled in Western Carolina University in Cullowhee, North Carolina.  He graduated in 2016 with a double major in English and Theatre performance.

The actor stands tall at a height of 6 feet and 2 inches (1.88 meters) and possesses a bodyweight of 174 pounds (79 kg).

Who Is Drew Starkey Dating?

Drew Starkey has not revealed his girlfriend and recent dating status.

The actor was previously rumored to be dating his Outer Banks co-star Madelyn Cline. Madelyn was living in an apartment below Drew which is why the two had been hanging out a lot during the coronavirus lockdown.

However, Madelyn officiated her romantic relationship with her other Outer Banks co-star, Chase Stokes back on 15 June 2020. The pair’s dating confirmation came weeks after their romance rumors began earlier in April 2020.

This news shovels Drew and Madelyn’s rumored relationship out of the park. On top of that, Drew previously dated a girl named Claire Van der Linden somewhere in 2014. Claire is known for her work in the 2019 psychological thriller film Knives and Skin.

His Movies & TV Shows; Love, Simon

Drew Starkey began his acting endeavors with the 2014 short movie, The Radical Notion of Gene Mutation. He later appeared in his other short movies like Lost Soles (2015) and Up the Hill (2016).

Drew recorded his major movie break with the 2018 comedy-romance movie titled Love, Simon. Following that, he appeared in several other movies like The Hate U Give (2018), Just Mercy (2019), The Devil All the Time (2020), and Embattled (2020).

On top of that, Drew ringed his television debut with the series, Mercy Street, in 2017. He then appeared on Ozark (2017), Doom Patrol (2019), and Scream (2019). His most promising performance was as Rafe in the 2020 Netflix adventure-mystery series, Outer Banks.
