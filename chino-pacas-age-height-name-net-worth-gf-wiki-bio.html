Career

Chino Pacas always had a dream of building a career in the music industry. He started writing his own songs and worked hard to improve his skills.

His talent as a rapper caught the attention of Street Mob Records, who offered him a record deal. On January 27, 2023, he made his debut with the single “El Gordo Trae El Mando.”

The song became an instant hit and even made it onto the Billboard Hot 100 chart in early April, reaching the #75 position. Before that, it had already reached #13 on Billboard’s Hot Latin Songs chart.

“El Gordo Trae El Mando” is by far Chino Pacas’ most popular song with nearly 150 million streams on Spotify. Just about a month later in February, he released his second single titled “Negro Como La Pantera” in collaboration with Group Calle 24, marking his first-ever musical collaboration.

In March, he teamed up with Fuerza Regida for the single “Dijeron Que No La Iba Lograr,” which also gained millions of streams and sales. Currently, it has accumulated over 55 million streams on Spotify and 42 million views on YouTube.

On April 14th, he released his latest single called “Los Verdes.” After collaborating consecutively with other artists, this was a solo release. In just a few months, he achieved success that is comparable to many established music artists.
